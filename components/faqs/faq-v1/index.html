Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly does Dr. Melasmin® do?

It supports smoother, brighter-looking under-eyes and helps improve the look of hollowing, dark circles, and crepey skin.

How fast will I see results?

Many customers notice a fresher look within days, with best results from consistent daily use.

How do I use it?

Glide gently under each eye for about 15 seconds. Use daily on clean skin before heavier creams or makeup.

Will it irritate my under-eye skin?

It is designed for delicate under-eye skin, but patch test first if you are very sensitive.

Is it safe for daily use?

Yes, it is made for daily use as part of your skincare routine.

Can I use it with other skincare?

Yes. Use it before heavier creams, sunscreen, or makeup.

Where is it made?

It uses Korean skincare-inspired actives and clean manufacturing standards.

What if it doesn’t work for me?
